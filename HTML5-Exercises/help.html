Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I register for an event?
    Scroll to the "Secure Event Registration" section, complete the required contact fields, choose your preferred category, and submit. An confirmation output will populate below the form.
  • What does the local caching system do?
    The portal utilizes standard browser localStorage to securely save your chosen event category. Upon subsequent page reloads, this preference is read and pre-selected. Click the "Reset Preferences" button to clean browser cache parameters.
  • Why does the portal request location data?
    Our distance mapping service accesses browser high-accuracy Geolocation coordinates to calculate the path of the nearest civic office. Your physical position data is processed locally and never transmitted to our databases.
  • How do I close the lightbox zoom?
    Double-click any community image to scale it. To close the overlay, either click the close button (×) at the top right, or click anywhere inside the blank dark background.

For further technical support, contact the civic administrator at admin@civiclink.gov
