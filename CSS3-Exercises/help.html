Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I test the responsiveness of the portal?
Open the page in Google Chrome, right-click anywhere, and select Inspect. In the developer tool pane, click the Device Toggle Toolbar icon at the top to simulate fluid screen layouts. Below 768px, the navigation links stack vertically, and event cards dynamically form a single column.
2. How does the interactive display vs visibility lab function?
Our laboratory section provides a live comparison:
  • visibility: hidden hides the middle card but keeps its structural bounding box active in the page layout.
  • display: none completely discards the card from the rendering flow, collapsing the third card to fill its space.
3. How are the newspaper columns styled?
The news bulletin is styled with native CSS3 columns: column-count: 2, column-gap: 40px, and a hairline divider column-rule: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.12). This simulates a high-end publication editorial look.
